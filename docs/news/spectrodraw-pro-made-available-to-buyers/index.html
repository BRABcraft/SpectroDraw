SpectroDraw Pro is Now Available to All Buyers

Published: Mar 24, 2026


SpectroDraw Pro is moving into its next phase, with a new change in how access is granted. Starting now, anyone who purchases SpectroDraw Pro for $40 will receive immediate, full access. This means there is no longer a wait period tied to the final release for paid users.

If you purchased SpectroDraw Pro, you can access it at spectrodraw.com/app. If you want to use the free version, then you can go to the same link while logged out. Please note that there may be a slight delay before the Pro version loads while our server checks if you purchased it.

This update is designed to make the experience more straightforward and rewarding for those who choose to support the project. Buyers can jump in right away and begin using Pro features as development continues.

For those who joined through the free early access program, your support has been incredibly valuable. That early momentum helped shape SpectroDraw into what it is today. Your access will remain active, and you will receive full Pro access once the public release is complete.
